RECORDING OF CLASSES AND ACTIVITIES POLICY
1. The University does not permit student to electronically record classes,
performances, conferences and similar activities without written permission.
2. Recording a class or any other university activity without permission may result in
the University taking disciplinary action under the Student Code of Conduct.
3. Students wishing to record a class, whether in audio or video, must email the
instructor to seek written permission. The instructor has the final decision on this,
and is not required to provide an explanation.
4. Students wishingto record a performance, conference or similar, whether in audio or
video, must email the organiser to seek written permission. Any recording must
comply with the University Privacy Notice and is subject to the university’s
obligations with regard to data protection. The organiser has the final decision on
this, and is not required to provide an explanation.
5. Should permission be granted, any recording may only be for private use and my
not be rebroadcast. Broadcasting or transmitting a class or similar, or an excerpt
from a class or similar, without permission, by any form of media, will be treated as a
serious offense under the Student Code of Conduct and will result in disciplinary
action being taken.
Exceptions:
6. Students with a special educational need or a disability which can be assisted by the
recording of a class must inform either Registry Services or Student Affairs and
provide them with further information. Any approach will be treated in strict
confidence. Registry Services or Student Affairs will make a determination on
whether recording is permitted, and on other areas where the university may be
able to provide support. When a decision is taken to allow recording, they will also
inform instructors that permission to record has been given and will offer you
additional support or make the arrangements with the instructor on your behalf. Any
recording allowed under this exception are for private use and may not be
rebroadcast.
7. Instructors delivering in-classroom or online sessions may wish to record sessions for
students who are unable to attend. Students are informed each semester that
