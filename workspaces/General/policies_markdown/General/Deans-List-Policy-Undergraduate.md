DEAN’S LIST POLICY: UNDEGRADUATE
1. Any undergraduate degree-seeking, Visiting, or Study Abroad student who achieves a
3.4 GPA in any Fall or Spring semester while taking three or more courses (9 US/36 UK
credits or more) will be placed on the Dean’s List for that semester. This recognition of
academic achievement is noted on the student’s academic transcript.
