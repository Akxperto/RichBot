APPICATION FOR GRADUATION AND CONFIRMATION OF DEGREE
POLICY
Graduation Audits
1. Undergraduates may not graduate without having a complete ‘Graduation Audit’
conducted by Registry Services to ensure that all degree requirements have been
completed.
2. A “Timeline for Graduating Seniors” is posted on the Registry Services page of the Portal
each semester, and an email is sent to all students informing them of their
responsibilities in this regard.
3. Students must submit an “Application for Graduation” form to Registry Services,
endorsed by their academic advisor, in the semester before they plan to complete their
degree requirements.
4. Registry Services will conduct an initial audit to ensure that graduation requirements will
have been fulfilled. Changes to a student’s registration may be required at this point.
5. A final graduation audit is carried out after grades have been submitted for the final
term of enrolment and a confirmation of completing degree requirements is sent from
Registry Services to qualifying students.
6. Students who have completed the University’s graduation requirements at any of the
three points in the year at which diplomas are issued are reported to the UKVI as having
completed their studies.
7. Students completing requirements outside of the Spring semester, who wish to return
and participate in the annual Congregation Ceremony in May, are welcome to do so
under a general visitor visa.
8. Undergraduate results are considered at the annual University Exam Board held in the
Summer (see Examinations Policy: Administration and Invigilation).
Students Completing Graduation Requirements Elsewhere
9. Students are normally expected to complete their graduation requirements at the
University. In exceptional circumstances, however, a student may apply to the Head of
Registry Services for permission to complete graduation requirements elsewhere under
an official Leave of Absence (see Leave of Absence Policy).
10.If official transcripts confirming completion of the pre-approved courses with grades of C
or better are received by Registry Services before the end date of the leave of absence,
