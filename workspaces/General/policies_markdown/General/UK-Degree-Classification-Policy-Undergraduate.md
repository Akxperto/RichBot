UK DEGREE CLASSIFICATION POLICY: UNDERGRADUATE
1. UK Honours degrees at undergraduate level are classified as first class (1st), upper
second class (2.1), lower second class (2.2) and third class (3rd).
2. Pass degrees are not classified as Honours degrees.
3. Exam Boards are not permitted to raise or lower the GPA of any candidate that is close
to a boundary, (sometimes referred to as “borderlines”).
4. Revision of grades is only permissible at the level of an individual course when based on
clear evidence that the grade does not accurately reflect the candidate’s performance.
5. The conversion table between the UK Honours degree classification and the UK is
summarised below:
Grade UK GPA Class
A/A- 4.000-3.550 1st
B+/B 3.549-3.000 2.1
B/B- 2.999-2.550 2.2
C+/C 2.549-2.000 3rd
C- 1.999-1.850 Pass
D+ to F 1.849-0.0 Fail
For students registered on the US and UK awards:
6. Classification of the degrees awarded to graduates are based upon an UK GPA. The UK
GPA is derived from grades awarded in a minimum of 30 US /120 UK credits completed
at Level 5 (excluding any L5 transfer credit) and 30 US/120 UK credits completed at Level
6.
7. Where more than 30 US/120 UK credits are required at these levels for completion of
requirements in the major, the UK GPA will include those additional credits
8.
9. The UK GPA is weighted, with grades awarded at Level 5 counting for 33.3% of the UK
GPA and grades awarded at Level 6 counting for 66.7% of the UK GPA.
10. All undergraduate students must obtain a major GPA of 2.0 and an overall cumulative
GPA of 2.0 in order to be awarded a US degree.
11. Students whose overall cumulative GPA is 2.0, but whose UK GPA is between 1.999 and
1.85 will be awarded a UK pass degree.

For students registered on UK-only awards:
12. UK GPA is derived from grades awarded in a minimum of 120 UK credits completed at
Level 6.
13. No courses completed before admission to the UK-only Level 6 Top-Up degree will count
towards the UK GPA.
14. Students whose UK GPA is between 1.999 and 1.85 will be awarded a UK pass degree.
