US LATIN HONORS POLICY: UNDEGRADUATE
1. This policy does not apply to UK-only top-up degrees.
2. Graduating students whose cumulative GPA is at least 3.4 and whose major GPA is at
least 3.5 may graduate with the following US Latin honors:
a. cum laude if their major GPA is at least 3.5
b. magna cum laude if their major GPA is at least 3.6
c. summa cum laude if their major GPA is at least 3.7
3. Minors are not eligible for US Latin honors.
4. US Latin honors are confirmed by the Academic Registrar and are listed on the US
degree diploma and transcript. For the avoidance of doubt, in the case of students with
double majors, the highest ranking US Latin honors will be listed on the diploma, and
both listed on the transcript.
