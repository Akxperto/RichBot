RIASA FEES POLICY
1. This policy does not apply to UK-only top-up degrees.
2. Fees for the Richmond International Academic Soccer Academy (RIASA) in Leeds are
payable in UK Pounds Sterling. Students are required to commit to the programme
financially for a full academic year.
3. The programme fee includes: academic and sports tuition, room/board for 35 weeks
(Fall and Spring Semesters for years 1 and 2) with the programme commencing in
August and ending in May.
4. For year 1 and 2 students, accommodation is based on single occupancy and 12 meals
per week. Additional meals beyond the 12 meals provided are available. Upon request,
at an additional fee, out of term accommodation is priced per week. Students may
occupy the housing facilities over the holiday breaks and may leave their belongings in
their residential halls during the 35-week programme.
5. A deposit of £1,000 is required of all new students. If a student fails to enrol, the
deposit is non-refundable and non-transferable. For new students requiring a
Confirmation of Acceptance for Studies (CAS ) and a student visa to study in the UK, the
full year fee is payable in advance, unless US Federal Loan funding has been certified by
the University’s Financial Aid Officer. Once enrolled, a full academic year of fees will
become due, payable in two instalments.
6. The payment deadlines are 1st July for the Fall Semester and 1st December for the
Spring Semester.
